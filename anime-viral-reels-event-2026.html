Phase 04 · Creator Tips
Creator Growth Zone
01

Hook in 0–3 Seconds

Start with the most intense frame of your edit. Never start cold — hit them with the best part FIRST. The algorithm rewards instant engagement.

02

Beat Sync is Everything

Time every cut, flash, and shake to the beat drop. Sync = retention. Retention = reach. CapCut's beat sync tool makes this effortless.

03

Use Trending Audio Early

Jump on viral sounds within 24–48 hours of trending. CapCut's trending library and TikTok sounds are your cheat code for algorithmic reach.

04

Reel Length: 7–15 Seconds

Short, punchy, and re-watchable. The algorithm rewards replays — edits that feel too short get rewatched. Keep them wanting MORE.

05

Faceless = More Scalable

Anime visuals + text overlays + AI voice means you can post daily with zero burnout. Build a system, not just a channel. Automate your growth.
